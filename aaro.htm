About AARO

The Advancement and Alumni Relations Office (AARO) is the central pillar for nurturing and enhancing the essential bonds between our distinguished university and its alumni, donors, and friends. Our mission is deeply rooted in creating a strong sense of community, instilling pride, and promoting lifelong engagement among our diverse network of graduates and supporters. In addition to fostering these connections, AARO also plays a crucial role in career placement, providing valuable resources and support to help soon-to-be-graduates and alumni achieve their professional goals. Through innovative programs, meaningful events, and strategic partnerships, we strive to ensure that our alumni and benefactors remain connected and integral to the continued success and growth of our university.

Our Functions:

Alumni Relations: We are dedicated to fostering enduring connections with our esteemed alumni community. Through meticulously planned events, targeted communications, and strategic networking opportunities, we celebrate the accomplishments of our alumni and provide platforms for them to maintain strong bonds with each other and their alma mater.

Fundraising and Development: In alignment with our commitment to the university's advancement, we spearhead fundraising initiatives to secure philanthropic support. Our team collaborates with donors, foundations, and corporate partners to fund scholarships, research projects, and other essential programs that enhance the university's influence and reach.

Stewardship: Our office is devoted to recognizing and expressing gratitude for the generosity of our donors. We prioritize maintaining robust relationships with our supporters, ensuring their contributions are acknowledged and demonstrating the significant impact of their philanthropy on the university community.

Special Events: We organize a variety of special events, from alumni reunions to donor recognition ceremonies, to bring our community together. These events serve as opportunities for celebration, networking, and expressing appreciation to those who contribute to the university's success.

Communications and Outreach: Keeping our stakeholders informed and engaged is a key priority. Our office produces newsletters, compelling social media content, and other materials to showcase the university's achievements and share impactful stories with our broader community.

Career and Placement Services: We support our alumni and current students in their professional development and career advancement. Through career counseling, job placement assistance, and professional development workshops, we strive to equip our community with the resources and connections needed to succeed in their chosen fields.
