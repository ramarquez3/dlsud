DLSU-D College Academic Calendar Schedule

Each school year in De La Salle University-Dasmariñas is divided into two terms. The first semester typically starts in August and ends in December. The second semester resumes in January and usually ends in May. The University also accepts enrollees for the special term covering the summer months of June to July.

DLSU-D High School Academic Calendar Schedule

The academic year for De La Salle University-Dasmariñas High School is divided into the first and second term. The first term covering the first and second quarter starts in August and ends in December while the second term covering the third and fourth quarters start in January and end in June.

DLSU-D College of Law Academic Calendar Schedule

The academic year 2024-2025 at the College of Law of De La Salle University-Dasmariñas is structured into three terms: the first term, the second term, and the special term. The first term will run from August 2025 to December 2025. The second term will commence in January 2026 and conclude in May 2026. Lastly, the special term will take place from June to August 2025.

DLSU-D Graduate Studies Academic Calendar Schedule

The academic year for De La Salle University-Dasmariñas Graduate Studies is divided into the first, second, and third trimesters. The first term covering the first and second quarter starts in September 2025 and ends in December 2025 while the second term covering the third and fourth quarters start in January 2026 and end in May 2026. Lastly, third term starts in May 2026 and ends in August of 2026.
