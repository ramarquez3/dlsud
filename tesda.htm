  About

The De La Salle University - Dasmariñas TESDA Assessment Center (DLSU-D TAC) is an assessment center for Technical-Vocational Education (TVE) that provides a support system in recognition and awards of certifications based on TESDA standards of assessment. A world-class and constructive work values conforming with the rules, policies and regulations of TESDA.

Vision

A leading technical-vocational assessment activator empowering skills that inspires transformation towards adequate society in the Province of Cavite.

Mission

To provide trustworthy and high quality assessment qualifications with green technology unification receptive to the needs of the business firms.

Goal

To produce proficient and efficient qualified skilled individual with green skills through contemporary facilities and quality TESDA delivery approach with globally competitive labor force.

Objectives

  • To provide assessment center to its own Basic Education grade 10 moving up and grade 12 graduates for their TESDA certification needs.
  • To develop green skilled graduates in tertiary level.
  • To intensify the institution's dimensions of quality and globally competitive certification programs.
  • To enhance the potentiality of the teachers/ professors.
  • To ameliorate the institution's assessment infrastructure.
  • To secure proficient and efficient assessment performance.
  • To widen linkages and partnerships with other schools and industries.
  • To provide assistance to out-of-school youth, ALS, and other less fortunate youth in taking TESDA certification.

Services Offered

  • We accept applicants and process the documentary requirements for TESDA National Certification [NC].
  • We serve as the Venue for NC Assessment.

List of available TESDA Qualifications

  • TPS - Tourism Promotion Services
  • CSS - Computer System Servicing
  • BKP - Bookeeping

Assesment Requirements:

  • 1 pc. Birth Certificate (photo copy)
  • 1 pc. Certificate of Employment for Industry worker (photo copy)
  • 1 pc. Training Certificate or TOR/COG with TESDA embedded curriculum for Students
  • 1 pc. Any Valid ID (photo copy)
  • Duly accomplished Application Form
  • Properly filled out Self-Assessment Guide [SAG]:
    • Bookkeeping NCIII
    • Tourism Promotion Services NCII
    • Computer Systems Servicing NCII - Full
    • Computer Systems Servicing NCII - COC1
    • Computer Systems Servicing NCII - COC2
    • Computer Systems Servicing NCII - COC3
    • Computer Systems Servicing NCII - COC4
  • Three (3) pieces of colored passport-size picture, white background, with collar and with NAME TAG at the bottom part of the picture (full name with middle initial)

Note: All documents should be printed in an A4 size bond paper, and to be placed in a LONG BROWN ENVELOPE. First come, first serve. Please complete the requirements first.

Rules on the Assesment Day

  • Arrive on or before the time based on the scheduled date on DLSU-D TAC venue of assessment as indicated in the Admission slip.
  • Bring assessment admission slip and valid ID bearing a photograph and signature.
  • In case of absence payment will be forfeited and considered used.
  • Full payment will be charged for re-assessment.
