How to get to DLSU-Dasmariñas

  • Home/
  • How to get to DLSU-Dasmariñas

Get in Touch

De La Salle University-Dasmariñas is considered the Calabarzon region's premiere university. With 27 hectares composed of period-inspired architectural structures surrounded by the beauty of nature, it is a unique learning environment that offers a relaxing theme park atmosphere with world class education spurred on by high impact research projects and technological innovations that ensure graduates' competencies for the workplace after completing their degree. Most, importantly, it is a University where the experience of God is lived and shared, the University's mantra that embodies the Lasallian values of Faith, Zeal and Communion.

Landmarks

  • You know you're getting close to the DLSU-D when you reach these places.
  • De Salle University - Health and Sciences Institute (2.1 kms away from Gate 1)
  • Kadiwa Park and Hanging Bridge (1.1 kms away from Gate 1)
  • Dasmariñas National High School (800 meters away from Gate 1)

Entrance Gate

  • Magdalo (Gate 1)
  • Magpuri (Gate 2) - vehicles only
  • Magdiwang (Gate 3)
  • Magtagumpay (Gate 4) - vehicles only
