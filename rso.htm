Recognized Student Organizations



Recognized Student Organizations refer to any organization of students duly recognized by the Accreditation Board and the CSO, operating pursuant to the rules and regulations set forth by the Student Development Activities Office (SDAO).

There are two kinds of RSO according to their nature:

Co-curricular Organizations

They are those that support the academic development of its members. The main thrust is based on the objectives and nature of the academic program each organization aims to complement.



1. Allied Business Students Collective (ABSCo)

The ABSCo is the premier Business Economics and Entrepreneurship organization of the De La Salle University - Dasmariñas which aims to foster an environment that supports business and economic growth, encourages innovation and entrepreneurship, and promotes sustainable practices.
2. Business and Operations Management Society (BOMS)

Mission: The Business and Operations Management Society is committed to making a positive impact in the community by upholding the organization’s mission and vision statements. BOMS has a clear purpose and set of objectives. It aims to create a strong co-curricular organization for all Business and Operations Management students, fostering unity and collaboration.

Vision: Its officers also focuses on developing leadership skills among its members to prepare them for management situations. Additionally, it encourages community outreach activities aligned with the Lasallian mission, promoting social responsibility. The organization supplements academic learning through educational seminars, providing additional knowledge and opportunities for growth. Overall, it strives to instill the values of faith, zeal, and communion while fostering a sense of Lasallian spirituality among its members.

3. DLSU-D Pre-Medical Society (DPMS)

Mission: The DLSU-D Pre-Medical Society shall continue its efforts, skills, and talents to promote community engagement and public health awareness as a viable profession through extracurricular activities. These movements will spark interest in its members to pursue and have passion for their careers as future health professionals.

Vision: The DLSU-D Pre-Medical Society embodies the ideals and aspirations of the members that promote and protect the welfare and interests of its members. The organization values youth empowerment, community, engagement, and public health awareness, which enables a high degree of intellectual growth in both the biological and medical fields.

4. DLSU-D Psychological Society (DPS)

ABOUT US

The DLSU-D Psychological Society is a co-curricular organization for Psychology students at De La Salle University Dasmariñas. Committed to its mission to offer opportunities for growth and advancement in both academic and non-academic activities, the DLSU-D Psychological Society aims to create a safe and inclusive community. Our goal is to contribute to the study of mind and human behavior while fostering an environment that supports these values.

MISSION: The DLSU-D Psychological Society (DPS) aims to educate future Lasallian Psychology Graduates by providing opportunities for further growth and advancement in academic and non-academic activities that encourage its members to use and apply their acquired knowledge and to deepen their understanding of the field of Psychology. The organization aspires to empower its members to campaign for their advocacies and bridge the gap between psychology and the community while striving for devotion, progress, and success by training them to become socially responsible citizens.

VISION: The DLSU-D Psychological Society (DPS) envisions well-rounded, highly competent, and socially responsible Lasallian Psychology Graduates devoted to serving individuals and our society, excelling in the field and all aspects of life as they apply their knowledge in psychological studies. They are empowered to establish a safe community that fosters inclusivity and diversity for the people while contributing to the study of the mind and human behavior in the field.

The DLSU-D Pre-Medical Society shall continue its efforts, skills, and talents to promote community engagement and public health awareness as a viable profession through extracurricular activities. These movements will spark interest in its members to pursue and have passion for their careers as future health professionals.

5. Hotel and Restaurant Management Society (HRMS)

Radiating Love for the Philippine Hospitality Industry through Sparkling Student Service.

6. Junior Marketing Association (JMA)

The De La Salle University - Dasmarinas Junior Marketing Association (DLSU-D JMA) is the mother marketing organization of DLSU-D duly uccredited Philippine Junior Marketing Association. DLSU-D JMA serves and promotes the continuous improvement of every Marketing and Advertising student by spearheading events and activities that would shape and equip their skills as future professionals themselves.

Through strong unity and dedication, the organication shall provide excellence and motivate deeper engagement of the Marketing and Advertising students that would generate them new knowledge and upplications for effective pructices, that would foster them towards the attainment of a better and productive society.

7. Lasallian Educators Society (LES)

MISSION: To develop inspiration amongst future Lasallian educators and the community to become transformative leaders who are motivated with compassion, empathy, and resolve to respond to educational needs under a supportive and collaborative environment.

VISION: To be a way of fostering knowledge and excellence. Nurturing the potential of each future educator by embodying the core values through faith, service, and communion. Empowering students to have a vision and purpose in other people's lives, to contribute to the world we live in, and to have compassion to thrive in all aspects of life.

8. Tourismo Lasalleño Society (TLS)

Tourismo Lasalleño Society is an organization established last December 4, 2002, to help the tourism industry in the Philippines pursue its goals even in a small way. Students from De La Salle University - Dasmariñas can benefit from this organization in various possible ways that the body can come up in union with its members. It was founded so that the students can take part in promoting international peace and understanding and to enable them to focus and be aware on the environmental issues and situations that concern our country so much even in the own portals of our Alma Mater. Mission TLS also helps students/members to make a stand, be competitive and participative in promoting the university, and can help to uplift the country's tourism industry and make a change for a better country, its future, and insights, and be Christian achievers for God. Vision TLS envisions itself as the leader, the invincible organization, and highly competitive among others

Interest Organizations

They are those that cater to the development of its members in a specific field of interest. Such interest shall be of significance to the member’s development of ethics, skills and talents.



1. Accounting Enrichment Society (ACES)

MISSION: The Accounting Enrichment Society exists to promote the general welfare of students taking accounting and finance subjects, to support the continuous program improvements by the accountancy department in achieving academic excellence, to provide information and coordinate events intended to stimulate their interest in accounting, finance, and a higher scholastic aim, to provide an opportunity for networking and sharing of practical information to attain a high degree of moral, social and intellectual growth, and to strengthen the common ties that bind them.

VISION: The Accounting Enrichment Society aims to provide its members with a high level of social, moral, and personality development, vital in their employment as accounting professionals. This envelops the academic demands of non-accountancy students pursuing Accounting and Finance classes, as well as those interested in learning the subject itself, in particular in the field of Accounting and Finance.
2. Circle of Student Assistants (CoSA)

The Circle of Student Assistants (CoSA) aims to be beneficial to the academic community in promoting an indolence-free environment, in encouraging more student scholars to uplift their educational standards in maintaining harmony and unity among fellow student assistants and the members of the Lasallian community, and in creating external linkages and outreach programs.

3. Communication and Media Society (CommSoc)

The DLSU-D Communication and Media Society, also known as COMMSOC, is a duly recognized university-wide organization open for Media and Communication enthusiasts that aims to hone and develop the technical and creative skills of its members, ensuring that passion goes hand in hand with academic excellence. And this year's theme is focused on amplifying voices of students in creating, connecting, and most importantly communicating.

4. DLSU-D Pre-Law Society(DPLS)

MISSION: The duty of DLSU-D Pre-Law Society is to foster and cultivate university-wide interest in the study of law. In addition, it also aims proficiency in this field-promoting respect for the rule of law, encourage academic excellence, develop its members to become committed advocates of the law and figurehead when it comes to fellowship among the members of the Lasallian community.

VISION: With the mission in their hearts, the Society hereby envisions an academic atmosphere that is involved in the study of law, where critical thinking prevails, potential and individuality are encouraged, and the members are seen active in educating others.
5. DLSU-D Lifters

“We Rise by Lifting Others”

Mission: Seek the lost and discipline the found. The organization aims to lift those who are weary, to carry each other's burdens, and to help uplift the student's spirit as they deal with emotional and spiritual needs. DLSU-D lifters is committed and grateful to our God almighty, who raises us up when we fail, and gives strength when cannot continue.

Vision: Promote an environment where, in the midst of a free-thinking world, students can openly share essential ideas, encourage their fellow Lasallians and experience uplifting conversations, and in turn, produce efficient and effective Christian professionals after its members leave the comforts of the academe world.

6. DLSU-D Patriots of Animal Welfare and Support (PAWS)

The DLSU-D Patriots of Animal Welfare and Support (DLSU-D PAWS) is the official student organization of animal welfare advocates, with a burning passion for raising awareness and providing assistance to animals in need. We promote protection, social awareness, camaraderie, empathy, and respect of animals, both domestic and wild. Guided by the Lasallian core values of Faith, Zeal for Service, and Communion in Mission

7. DLSU-D United Patriots Football Club (DUPFC)

Preamble “We are the DLSU-D (De La Salle University Dasmariñas) United Patriots, a football club striving to excel on the field while maintaining the principles of good sportsmanship. We come together in the spirit of teamwork, respect, and friendship. Here, we celebrate not just winning but also the pure enjoyment of playing the game that we love under the Lord's constant guidance and grace." Vision We envision a football community built on integrity, inclusivity, and continuous growth, where players are empowered to lead both on and off the field. United by a love for the sport, we aim to create a diverse, supportive environment that encourages positive contributions to the team and the wider community. Mission Our mission is to develop skilled, well-rounded football players by recruiting talent, enhancing their abilities, and promoting physical fitness. We foster leadership, teamwork, and the Lasallian spirit of service, while also engaging in outreach programs to uplift and inspire youth beyond our campus.

8. Junior Financial Executives Institute of the Philippines - DLSU-D (JFINEX)

The Junior Financial Executives Institute of the Philippines - De La Salle University-Dasmariñas (JFINEX - DLSU-D) is the premier finance organization of the institution, with a growing community of 9.6k likes and 9.9k followers on their official Facebook page. JFINEX - DLSU-D provides avenues for students towards unexplored possibilities as they adhere to their advocacy of promoting financial literacy and awareness. The organization fosters an open community with enriched atmosphere between students, organizations, and businesses sharing similar advocacies. JFINEX - DLSU-D is committed to providing academic advancement and social responsibility, ensuring to further the expertise of their fellow students in the field of business, banking, and finance.

9. Marché Société of DLSU-Dasmariñas (MS)

Marché Société, a French term for “Marketing Society” is a recognized student interest organization that serves as an instrument to discover people who have the potential, to hone their skills, and interests, particularly marketing students through leadership and responsibility for creating and distributing “MarchÉ” the official student news magazine publication formed under the Marketing Department of the College of Business Administration and Accountancy at De La Salle University – Dasmariñas.

10. PROJECT: Ikigai (PROJ:Ik)

Here at Project: Ikigai, we welcome each and every individual searching for a place to let their creativity and skills thrive in a community that is able to appreciate everyone's presence no matter their hobbies and interests. The heart of the organization is built upon the social aspect and interaction amongst each faction that being the artists, gamers, and cosplayers. It is this same community of people that gives life to the organization and will only continue to flourish with everyone's contribution towards creating a space where anyone is free to stay. Together, may we all work towards discovering our meaning, our Ikigai!

11. SINAG Society of Leaders (SISOL)

A place to develop your skills; A sanctuary to heal what's broken; An organization to call HOME. Mission: To develop genuine and excellent student leaders, and encourage students to become active members of society. Vision: To be the premier leadership organization that creates excellent life-long leaders.


Want to form an Organization?

Students who would want to form an organization can apply every Summer Term (within fifteen (15) working days from the start of summer classes) and first semester (within fifteen (15) working days after the last day of Midterm Examinations). Requirements to be submitted include the following:

  • Letter of application addressed to the director of SDAO through the SAO Coordinator.
  • A copy of the constitution and by-laws of the organization to be defended during the deliberation period. Such constitution and by-¬laws must not be contrary to the Vision-Mission of the University and the constitution of CSO.
  • Roster of officers/founders with the following information:
  1. Position
  2. Name
  3. Course, Year and Section
  4. E-mail Address
  5. Contact Number
  6. Name of Faculty Adviser, his/her academic rank, his/her College and Department and contact number

Note: The above requirement should be prepared by the president and noted by the adviser.

  • Recent Certificate of Good Moral Character of officers/founders.
  • Adviser's form (Form A) and Adviser’s Information Sheet to be filled out by the faculty adviser.
  • List of proposed plan of activities
  • Student Leader Information Sheet, photocopy of their current registration form and previous semester’s individual report of grades.
  • Recent Certificate of Good Moral Character of founding officers/Executive Board.
  • Forms can be found under the re-accreditation folder on this link: http://bit.ly/cso_forms.
