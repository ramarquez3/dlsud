Engineering Department

The Engineering Department is one of the departments under the College of Engineering, Architecture and Technology tasked with guiding students enrolled in BS Civil Engineering, BS Computer Engineering, BS Electronics Engineering, BS Electrical Engineering, BS Industrial Engineering, BS Mechanical Engineering and BS Environmental and Sanitary Engineering.

Faculty members, both full time and faculty are armed with years of experience to guide aspiring engineers. Their efforts, combined with the students' drive have resulted in a high passing rate for the University in the PRC board exams.

Vision

The Engineering Department envisions its graduates to become competent and responsible engineers who are committed to the highest ethical and professional conduct with social consciousness and environmental awareness that empower others as an agent of meaningful advocacies.

Mission

As a catalyst of social transformation, the department provides an excellent education that upholds and advances the integrity, honor and dignity of the engineering profession through relevant, responsive and community oriented curricula, research and extension services.
